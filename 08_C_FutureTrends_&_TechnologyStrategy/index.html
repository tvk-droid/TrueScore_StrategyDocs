  • Competitors starting with single-sensor face 12–18 months rearchitecting

The 95% Cost Reduction That Isn't Ready Yet.

Method Cost Quality Timeline

Synthetic (2030 projection) $5K–$10K 95–99% realism 2030
MATURITY GAP

Both required technologies — physics-based motion simulation and photorealistic video generation — are individually at 'Peak of Inflated Expectations.' They need to converge for cheerleading-specific applications. Current text-to-video cannot produce biomechanically accurate stunts.

FOUNDATION DATA

Collecting 100–500 real cheerleading videos as foundation data. Required regardless — for fine-tuning any synthetic pipeline, for few-shot pre-training, and for validation.

Go/No-Go (2027): Synthetic video >90% realism AND synthetic-trained models within 5% accuracy of real-data models.
